What is Bullet Time?

Bullet Time is a mobile multi-camera photo studio for instant creation of branded animations with the effect of "fading" time - changing the angle of view from camera to camera with gluing into a single video.

  • We generate more social media activity around your brand.
  • We cause the most powerful wow effect.
  • We collect a client base from email addresses, to which ready-made animations are sent!
  • We are very compact. The minimum area for the Bullet Time photo zone to work is 3x3 meters, the recommended one is 4x4 meters.

We work all over Europe. With the participation of our interactive entertainment, more than 300 events: from weddings and corporate events to federal-scale advertising campaigns and major international forums.

Suitable events for bullet time use

We have already set up a photo booth for nearly a thousand private and social events. Based on this experience, we have developed ready-made packages for photo booths for different occasions:

  • Wedding
  • Party or youth event
  • Birthday
  • Event of your company
  • Concerts
  • Charity events

Bullet is suitable for a wide variety of events, both for your personal and marketing events for your business.

You can familiarize yourself with the process, how we work during and after the event

  • Our assistant supplies and assembles the photo booth, tests and adjusts it to ensure the best photo and video quality. In addition, one of our staff can stay on site for a photo shoot
  • All content created by our bullettime can be shared with guests directly from the venue via text message, email, Bluetooth, QR code, or uploaded directly to social media
  • After the event, we will share a link to a gallery where all content is securely stored. We can also create a microsite using your brand and host a gallery there

Contact us if you have any questions. We will be happy to answer them.
